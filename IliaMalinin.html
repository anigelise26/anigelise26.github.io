Welcome! Here you can explore information about Ilia Malinin, including his career, achievements, and impact on figure skating, along with highlights of his performances and background.

  • OCCUPATION: Ilia Malinin is a professional figure skater whose job is to train daily on and off the ice, perform highly technical and artistic routines, and compete in national and international events representing the United States; his responsibilities include mastering complex jumps (including quadruple elements), maintaining peak physical condition, collaborating with coaches on choreography, traveling for competitions, and participating in media, sponsorships, and exhibitions as a public-facing elite athlete.
  • FUN FACTS: -First skater to land a quadruple Axel in competition -Nicknamed the “Quad God” -Both parents were competitive figure skaters -Represents the United States -Has Uzbek heritage -Became a top-level skater as a teenager -Known for performing multiple quadruple jumps in one program -Enjoys video games and anime outside of skating
  • IMPACT: Ilia Malinin has reshaped figure skating in a way that feels both inspiring and transformative, proving that limits in the sport are meant to be broken. His historic quadruple Axel and fearless approach to difficulty have not only raised the technical standard, but also sparked excitement and belief among fans and skaters alike. By combining bold ambition with creativity on the ice, he has helped usher in a new era where pushing boundaries is no longer rare, but expected.
