Basic Information

Learn about Ilia Beauty, a clean makeup brand that combines skincare and cosmetics, known for its innovative formulas, commitment to clean ingredients, and influence on the modern beauty industry.

  • OCUPATION: Ilia Beauty is a clean beauty brand that creates makeup products blending skincare and cosmetics, focusing on innovative formulas, safe ingredients, and modern, everyday-friendly beauty. Its occupation is designing and producing high-performance, skin-conscious makeup while promoting transparency and sustainability in the beauty industry.
  • FUN FACTS: -It was founded by a former model and makeup artist, combining professional insight with skincare science. -Known for blurring the line between makeup and skincare, creating products that actually care for your skin. -Emphasizes clean, non-toxic ingredients without compromising performance. -Its products are cruelty-free and often vegan, appealing to conscious consumers. -The brand has a cult following for its natural-looking, radiant finishes. -Ilia has been recognized for innovative packaging and modern design, making the products both functional and stylish.
  • FORMULAS: Ilia Beauty products stand out by combining makeup with skincare, using clean, non-toxic ingredients that hydrate and protect the skin while delivering color and coverage. Unlike conventional makeup, their formulas avoid harsh additives, making them gentler and healthier for everyday wear.
  • RECOMENDATIONS: ILIA True Skin Serum Foundation – A lightweight foundation with buildable medium coverage that blurs imperfections while nourishing skin over time thanks to soothing ingredients like niacinamide and allantoin. ILIA Limitless Lash Mascara – A cult‑favorite clean mascara that lifts, lengthens, and defines lashes without clumping, flaking, or irritating sensitive eyes—award‑winning for its performance and lash‑care ingredients.
